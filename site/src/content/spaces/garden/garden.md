The garden is one of Olrig Bank’s defining features. Its broad lawn, mature trees and colourful seasonal planting provide room for morning coffee, outdoor games or a quiet drink at the end of the day.

When the Main House and Cottage are booked separately, guests may share the garden. A whole-property booking has use of the garden as one group.
