Olrig Bank Cottage is a self-contained part of the original Victorian property. Its private entrance opens into a spacious split-level hall, with the kitchen and lounge beyond and the bedrooms and bathroom facilities upstairs.

The Cottage is designed for relaxed stays throughout the year. Guests can cook and eat together in the retro kitchen, settle beside the wood-burning stove in the lounge, or spend time outside in the large garden.
