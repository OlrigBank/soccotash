The Cottage provides a separate place to stay within Olrig Bank. It has its own entrance, a lounge and kitchen, two bedrooms, a bathroom, a separate WC, landing and mezzanine area.

It suits families or small groups who want independent accommodation while remaining close to Kendal town centre. The large garden is shared when the Main House and Cottage are occupied by separate bookings.
