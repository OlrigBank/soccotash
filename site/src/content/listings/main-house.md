Olrig Bank Main House combines the scale and character of a detached Victorian home with the practical facilities needed for a comfortable group stay. High ceilings, period details and generous windows give the shared rooms a light and spacious feel, while the lounge and dining room provide relaxed places to spend time together.

The four bedrooms accommodate eight guests across two king-size beds, one double bed and two single beds. Each bedroom provides a quieter place to retreat after a day in Kendal or exploring the Lake District.
