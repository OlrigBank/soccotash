# Description

## Introductory description

    Our charming self-contained two bedroom cottage is part of Olrig Bank, a large detached Victorian property in a 
    quiet, leafy, peaceful part of Kendal. 

    Guests enjoy the use of a large garden, free off-road parking and being only a short walk from the centre of a 
    historic market town. Whether you are exploring the Lake District, enjoying evenings by the fire, cooking in the 
    retro kitchen, or simply relaxing in the garden, the cottage offers a calm and comfortable retreat throughout 
    the year

## Indoor

    The cottage private entrance opens into a spacious split-level entrance hall with room for coats, walking boots and luggage.

    Across the hall a short staircase connects to the kitchen and the living room beyond.

    An open staircase leads upstairs to the mezzanine area and beyond that the bedrooms and bathrooms.

### Ground floor

 
#### Rooms

    Kitchen: The retro kitchen/diner evokes a sense of nostalgia. The lighting casts a warm glow, and while cooking, 
    you look out over the garden. There is an abundance of workspace, with everything needed for the relaxed 
    preparation and cooking of shared meals. 

    The kitchen is intentionally simple and sociable, designed around conversation, cooking and slow evenings together.

    Living Room: The bright and comfortable living room overlooks the garden and the new pedestrian bridge across the River Kent. 
    High ceilings, soft natural light and a contemporary wood burner create a calm and welcoming atmosphere in every season.
    Light the fire, settle into the sofa with a book or glass of wine, and enjoy a quiet evening after a day exploring the Lakes.



### Upstairs

## Bedrooms

    Both share an impressive view over the garden, the new bridge spanning the River Kent and the distant hills that 
    surround the town beyond. Natural light and the cottage’s quiet setting make them restful places to unwind 
    and have wonderful night's sleep.

    Bedroom 1: Comfortable king-size bed, chest of drawers, television and hanging space for clothes.

    Bedroom 2 Furnished with two comfortable single beds. Together with a chest of drawers, television and hanging 
    space for clothes, it is ideal for children, friends or additional guests.

    
## Bathroom & Toilet

    Bathroom: Recently renovated, it is spacious and contemporary in style, including a bath with both rainfall 
    and hand-held shower fittings and a washbasin, mirror and storage cabinet for toiletries.

    Toilet: For additional convenience and privacy, the toilet is a separate room, close to the bathroom, 
    with washbasin with hot running water.


### General
    Both bathrooms include complimentary toiletries, to ensure a comfortable stay.

## Outdoor

### Garden

    A beautiful, expansive private garden that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful spot to relax or space for outdoor fun, the garden is a standout feature of this property.
    
    The garden boasts a large green lawn, mature trees, and vibrant seasonal blooms - including striking rhododendrons and camellias that create a burst of colour in the spring and summer.
    
    There’s plenty of space for morning coffee on the grass, outdoor play, or evening drinks under the  sometimes clear Cumbrian skies. A greenhouse and stone walls add a charming, rustic feel to this outdoor retreat.

### Parking
    Convenient off-street parking for 2 vehicles.
