# Description

## Introductory description

Welcome to our stunning 4-bedroom detached Victorian home, nestled in the heart of Kendal, 
often called the gateway to the Lake District. This elegant property combines timeless 
period charm with modern amenities, creating an ideal retreat for families or groups 
looking to relax in one of England’s most picturesque regions.

## Indoor

### Ground floor 

    Step into a grand and welcoming entrance hall featuring beautiful Victorian tiled flooring, rich wooden details, and a striking staircase 
    leading to the upper floors. This area sets the tone for the home's timeless elegance and provides a warm and stylish first impression for guests

#### Rooms

    Lounge: A beautifully spacious room with high ceilings, ornate Victorian detailing, and large windows letting in plenty of natural light, 
    while providing a cosy and inviting atmosphere in the evenings.
    The lounge features plush sofas, armchairs, a decorative fireplace, and a large bookcase filled with reading matter for all tastes and ages.

    Dining Room: A warm and elegant space with a large dining table, seating for up to 8 guests, and charming Victorian décor. 
    The room is bathed in natural light from expansive windows and features a decorative fireplace, creating the perfect atmosphere 
    for shared meals and celebrations.

    Kitchen: A fully functional kitchen with a blend of traditional and modern charm. Featuring warm wooden cabinetry, plenty of counter space, and all the modern appliances needed for your stay. It’s a perfect setting for cooking and sharing meals.

    Bathroom 2: Compact yet stylish, this bathroom features a modern walk-in shower, sink, toilet and charming tiled floor.

## Outdoor

### Garden

    A beautiful, expansive private garden that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful spot to relax or space for outdoor fun, the garden is a standout feature of this property.
    
    The garden boasts a large green lawn, mature trees, and vibrant seasonal blooms - including striking rhododendrons and camellias that create a burst of colour in the spring and summer.
    
    There’s plenty of space for morning coffee on the grass, outdoor play, or evening drinks under the  sometimes clear Cumbrian skies. A greenhouse and stone walls add a charming, rustic feel to this outdoor retreat.

## Guest Access

    The Olrig Bank consists of the Main House and Cottage. Guests have access to all of the rooms in main house but not the cottage. The linking door between the 2 is locked from both sides. Guests have access to the whole of the garden and exclusive use of all the parking.
